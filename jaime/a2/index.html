Recipe: Mac and cheese

I chose Macaroni and cheese as my recipe because it was the first food I learnt how to make it and I still make it to this very day. I learnt it in grade 5, but in grade 8 (foods) I learnt how to make it better. In my house, I am the only one who knows this recipe which makes this recipe a bit special for me. Mac and Cheeese is also one of my favorite foods, as it is easy to make and it takes very little effort. Ingredients:

Ingredients:

  • Butter (45mg)
  • Flour (45mg)
  • 1 serving of macaroni
  • Water
  • 4 pinches of sal
  • Milk (10⅓cups)
  • Shredded cheddar cheese (6 cups)
  • Pinch of dried mustard
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Bread crumbs (30mg)

Steps:

  1. Boil salted water in a pot on 500°F
  2. After it starts boiling, put your pasta in.
  3. In a separate pot, put your butter and flour and heat on medium heat.
  4. Mix until it is one thing.
  5. Then pour in 3 cups of milk and stir for a minute.
  6. While stirring, put the rest of the milk in.
  7. Whisk for a minute then let it heat on medium/low heat.
  8. Put in all your cheese and whisk until it is melted and mixed.
  9. Then put 2 pinches of salt and one pinch of pepper.
  10. Put in the dried mustard and let it heat until it is completely mixed.
  11. When the pasta and the sauce are done put them on a plate/bowl and put the bread crumbs on top.
  12. Eat!
