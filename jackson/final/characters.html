Zombies

Characters

There are multiple characters in Black Ops II Zombies, the main ones are Edward Richtofen, a Nazi scientist who created weapons during the war, and loved to torture various victims of his. Nikolai Belinski, a Russian soldier who fought in the Red Army, he is highly violent even by the standards of soldiers, and is the second most evil next to Richtofen. Tank Dempsey, an American Marine who fought in the United States Marine Corps, He thought highly of himself and his country, such as having a preference of using American made weapons. And last but not least Takeo Masaki, a Japanese soldier who fought in the Imperial Japanese Army, and became a bloodthirsty Japanese killer who uses WWII as an excuse to murder people.
