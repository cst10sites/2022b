Introduction
Vince Carter was a basketbal player in the NBA between the years 1998-2020. To most fans, he is the greatest dunker of all time. Vince Carter was born on January 26, 1977 in Dayton Beach, Florida. As a high school player, Vince Carter was named an All American. After high school, Vince Carter went to the University of North Carolina and played as a college player for 3 years. He played college from 1995-1998. He was later drafted by the Golden State Warriors and traded to the Toronto Raptors on draft day. After his 22 year career, he is now an an NBA analyst for ESPN.
