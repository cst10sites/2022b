Best Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

I chose a chocolate chip recipe which is claimed to be the best chocolate chip cookies. I chose it because it says it’s the best recipe in the world. What does it tell you about me? Well, it tells you that I think cookies taste good.

The following amounts are for 24 cookies
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175°c.
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water, then, Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
