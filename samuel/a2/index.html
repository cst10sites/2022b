My Protein Shake Recipe

A recipe I know is one for a protein shake. You need to add milk to it, protein powder, and then some fruit of your choice, to basically make it whatever you like it to be. I chose this because it is a simple recipe I know and one that comes to my head. The recipe values simplicity and efficiency as it is an easy recipe, yet it is good for filling you up after some exercise.

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of Milk
  • 1 Banana
  • 1½ cup of Protein powder
  • 1 apple
  • 1¾ scoop of peanut butter
  • Other fruit or vegetable of your choice
  • Blender
  • A glass or mug to pour the drink when finished

Steps:

  1. Cut the banana into smaller peices
  2. Put the banana peices in the blender
  3. Cut up an apple into smaller slices
  4. Put the apple slices into the blender
  5. Pour roughly 6 cups of milk (more or less depending on how much you would like to make)
  6. Add any other foods you'd like in there
  7. Turn on the blender and blend the shake for about 1 minute until the shake is nice and liquid
  8. Pour the drink into a glass

Now you are finished and can enjoy your beverage!
