This recipe is how to make brownies. I picked this one because they are my favorite and I make them all the time at home, and it is a good recipe that is hard to mess up because it is pretty simple. And it also does not take a lot of ingredients or time to make, and most people already have at least half at home.

What you will need

  • ½ cups butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened coco powder
  • ½ cup of flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking-powder
