Easter Eggs

The hidden Easters Eggs are probably the coolest part of Zombies, the best Easter Egg is hidden in Mob of the Dead. This Easter Egg is called Pop Goes The Weasel, and it is the continuation of the No One Escapes Alive easter egg. The idea is to find hidden parts inside the Alcatrez Prison and build a plane to escape away from the zombies. The reason why it is so liked is how fun each part is to find and the aftermath of the plane escape. Another Easter Egg that is just as good is the Orgins Easter Egg which features the characters I talked about earlier. The name of it is called Little Lost Girl, and the objective is to build all the staff parts which was hidden by maxis. The lost girl your looking for is Maxis daughter Samantha who explains what to do and how to free her from agartha.
