Assignment 2: My Recipe

The recipe I chose is an old recipe for crumble topping, which I chose because I like what it can make and it is a recipe that my family has used for more than four generations.

Very Old Crumble Topping:

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup flour
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup oats

Method:

  1. Mix the sugar and flour in a food processor.
  2. Cut the butter into small cubes and add slowly, being careful not to overmix.
  3. Add oats and mix slightly. Do not blend the oats.
