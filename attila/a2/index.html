Latin Nachos

For today’s recipe we are explaining how to make a colorful, delicious tray of nachos just like how it is in Latin America. It is a quick recipe that takes maximum 15-20 minutes. You must first prepare all your ingredients.

Ingredients

• Cup of diced red pepper • Half a cup of diced shallots • 1.5 cups or 2 cups of shredded cheddar • Full tray of tortilla chips

1. Start by slicing your green onions into 5-10mm thick slices and by dicing your shallots and red pepper. 2. If you have a sue chef working with you let him/her grate the cheese or do it yourself with your preferred kind (cheddar works well). 3. Mix all your chopped ingredients in a bowl and spread evenly on your tray of tortilla chips. 4. Toss in the oven for 7min 350 degrees Fahrenheit. (optional) Dipping’s can be mixed sour cream with salt, or guacamole – smashed avocadoes with lime salt and pepper Do this while the Nachos cook
