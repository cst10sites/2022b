Kropsua - a delicious recipe!

This is a recipe that my family loves, Kropsua! It's a great meal to have for breakfast on the weekends with blueberry or strawberry jam. This recipe has 8 servings per meal. The total time it should take is about 1½ hours so make sure you have the time to make it.


The ingredients you will need are as following:

  • 200 grams of all purpose flour
  • 30 grams of butter
  • 350 grams of smoked bacon
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 cups of milk
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • Favorite jam

Now let's start making it!

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  2. Cook the bacon until it's chewy but not crispy, then slice it into squares roughly 1cm to a side.
  3. Crack the eggs into a large bowl then add the milk and salt to the bowl and whisk it till it's smooth.
  4. Pour the flour in and stir until it's all thoroughly mixed.
  5. Heat a pan (preferably cast iron) and melt the butter then spread it around until it covers the entire pan.
  6. Pour the liquid mixture of the bowl into the pan and then add the pieces of bacon on top evenly.
  7. Put the pan into the oven and bake for 45 mins or until the center turns a golden brown.
  8. Pull it out and wait for it to cool, this should take about 20 mins.
  9. Cut it into slices and eat warm with your favorite jam!

About this recipe

This is a meal that my family has almost every weekend and since it has so many serving, we have some for lunch through out the week. It's a Finnish recipe tracing back all the way to my great grandmother who gave us this recipe. I love to imagine how my father, my grand father, and my great grandmother where all making this recipe.
