RECIPE

My perfect recipe is for mac and cheese. I chose this recipe because i like simple things but if you add to the simple things you get great results.

prep
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1/2 tbsp of butter
  • 1/2 cups of milk

instructions
  1. boil water at 100 C
  2. boil the pasta for 7-8 min
  3. drain the water from the pasta
  4. mix the cheese sause,butter,and milk with the pasta
  5. Enjoy!
