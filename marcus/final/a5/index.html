Lets start with some bad jokes!

Why are stairs so suspicious?

Because they're always up to something!

You see, this joke is funny because while you could think 'literally' in the way that stairs always lead up to something. (Stairs, doors, etc.)

But the way the joke words it out is that the stairs are up to something in a mischievous way.





What did the janitor say when he jumped out of the closet?

Supplies!

You see, this is funny because when people jump out to scare their friends, they usually yell out 'Surprise!':.

([sur·prise] = an unexpected or astonishing event, fact, or thing)

When the janitor does it though, he yells out 'Supplies!' as he was in a supply closet at the time.



What do you call a fake noodle?

An im-pasta!

You see, this joke is funny because it's a play on words from the hit-game 'Among us'

In Among us, there is an Imposter:

([im·pos·tor] = a person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others, especially for fraudulent gain.)

In the joke, there is a noodle (pasta) that is not like the others, like the imposter in Among us. We call it an Im-pasta because it is an imposter among other pasta.
