Introduction:

Mojito is the most refreshing and ideal cocktail, stripped down to just the bare essentials of, lime juice, sugar, soda water, and mint. Each ingredient is specifically chosen to cure hot weather-induced struggles of thirst. This cocktail is sweet, acidic, minty, and sparkling perfect for the hot summer days.


Ingredients

  • ¼ Cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 Tablespoons honey
  • 6 Fresh mint leaves
  • 4oz Club soda/sparkling water
  • ½ Cup ice
  • Fresh or frozen berries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place mint leaves and lime juice in a glass and muddle them together. Muddling = mushing them up and crushing the leaves to release the flavor and oils the mint. You can either use a muddler or the handle of a wooden spoon.
  2. Add honey
  3. Add the sparkling water of your choice
  4. Garnish with lime slices, mint, and/or berries
