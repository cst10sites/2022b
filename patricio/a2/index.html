Hamburger

I chose a hamburger recipe, because I have always liked burgers. Burgers have protein, carbohydrates and fats, which means they provide me with all the sources of energy I require . The meat is very tasty when cooked right, and when it is mixed with the taste of vegetables and bread, it tastes even better. Burgers are also really simple, but still fulfill their task of making you feel full after you eat them.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • ½teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup fine dry breakcrumbs
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • Desired condiments

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg, salt and pepper. Place ground beef and breadcrumbs into the mixture. With hands or a fork, mix until well blended. Form into 4 patties approximately ¾ inch thick.
  3. Place patties on the prepared grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
  4. Take the patties out of the grill and put them in the buns with the condiments you want.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
