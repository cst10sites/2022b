Introduction

I like to watch TV and movies a lot, and I love to eat popcorn while watching. It's a great snack food, and since corn is a vegetable, popcorn is technically healthy ish. On this website, I will show you a good recipe for stovetop popcorn.

Stovetop Popcorn

Ingredients

Here is what you'll need:

  • 3 tablespoons of coconut oil or olive oil
  • ⅓ cup of popcorn kernels
  • 1 ½ tablespoon of butter
  • ½ tablespoon of salt
  • (optional: ½ cup of shredded cheese)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a thick-bottomed saucepan on medium high heat (150-200°C). If you are using coconut oil, allow all of the solid oil to melt.
  2. Put 3 or 4 popcorn kernels into the oil and wait for the popcorn kernels to pop.
  3. When the kernels pop, add the rest of the popcorn kernels to the saucepan.
  4. Cover the pot, remove from heat and count 30 seconds.
  5. Return the pan to the heat, it should begin popping all at once since we heated everything for 30 seconds.
  6. Gently shake the pan as the kernels pop.
  7. Once the popping slows to several seconds between pops, remove the pan from the heat
  8. Pour the popcorn into a bowl.
  9. Put the butter in the already hot pan to melt it.
  10. Pour the butter onto your popcorn, and add the salt.
  11. If you're weird like me, add some shredded cheese to the popcorn for fun, or add toppings of your choice.

Bon appetit!
