Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • ¼ cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • ¼ cup of melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 bowl
  • 1 Sifter
  • 1 Mixer or 1 whisk
  • 1 greased mold

Instructions

  1. Pour 2 cups of flour into one bowl
  2. Pour 2 cups of sugar into the bowl
  3. Sift ¼ cup of cocoa powder into the bowl
  4. Add 1 of teaspoon of salt into the bowl
  5. Add 1 of teaspoon baking powder into the bowl
  6. Add 2 of teaspoons baking soda into the bowl
  7. Use a whisk or a mixer to mix the batter
  8. Add 1 of cup buttermilk into the bowl
  9. Add 1 cup of hot water into the bowl
  10. Use a whisk or a mixer to mix the batter
  11. Add ¼ cup melted butter into the bowl
  12. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence into the bowl
  13. Crack three eggs into the bowl
  14. Use a whisk or a mixer to mix the batter
  15. Pour batter in a greased mold
  16. Tap the mold to release the air bubbles
  17. Bake for 45 minutes at 200 °C
  18. Let it cool completely when finishing baking
