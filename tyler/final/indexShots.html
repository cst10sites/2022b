Shots of soccer

There are many types of shots in soccer. 1. the straight shot. To do the straight shot you must strike the ball with the laces of your boot. Always looking at where you want the ball to go and strike the ball in a fluid motion. Your foot must flow through the ball and not stop as soon as you make contact with the ball.

2. the curve shot. To do the curve shot you must strike the ball at an angle and hit the ball on the side to give it the curve. The ball should make contact with the inside of your foot.

3. The chip shot. The chip shot is a goalies worst nightmare because if they get scored on via a chip, they will look very foolish. to do the chip shot you must hit the bottom of the ball with the tip of your boot. when performing a chip shot your foot should stop after you make contact with the ball or else you will end up scooping the ball upwards and losing controll.

4. the outside shot. the outside shot takes a lot of skill and a lot of practice to master,and thats because its very difficult to pull off. to do the outside shot you have to strike the ball with the outside part of your foot and curve the ball the opposite way as a curve shot. This shot is very difficult to do but if done correctly it looks absolutly brilliant.
