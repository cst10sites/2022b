Vince Carter

Teams

Vince Carter had a long career in the NBA. During his time in the NBA, he played for eight different teams. The teams were:

Toronto Raptors 1999-2005
New Jersey Nets 2005-2009
Phoenix Suns 2010-2011
Orlando Magic 2010-2011
Dallas Mavericks 2011-2014
Memphis Grizzlies 2014-2017
Sacramento Kings 2017-2018
Atlanta Hawks 2018-2020
