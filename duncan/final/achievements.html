Lionel Messi

Achievements

Messi has won a record seven Ballon d'Or awards, a record six European Golden Shoes, and in 2020 was named to the Ballon d'Or Dream Team. He has also won the 2009 FIFA World Player of the Year and 2019 The Best FIFA Men's Player Some other amazing achievements that Messi has won over the years are the Fifa World Player Of The Year, The Best Fifa Mens Player and the Pichichi Trophy
